FatBabble
Senior Thesis

Project Overview

FatBabble is the name for a self-initiated website designed to combat the lack of information surrounding the impact of fat talk — or fat babble. This project stemmed from research pertaining to the impact that advertising has on body image.

Role

Research, research organization, art direction, and production.
