Japan Itinerary Launch
Adventures by Disney

The Ask

To create video and social content to announce the launch of a Japan trip with Adventures by Disney in a way that was respectful of the island nation's rich culture & tradiitions (using existing B-roll and testimonials).

Solution

Our two lead videos told a clear linear narrative and custom music was scored to the pieces, music that melded uplifting indie music with authentic Japanese instruments. In the end, we created a new genre that did not exist before. To convey the brand messaging that Adventures by Disney helps you understand and experience cultures, we translated the Adventures by Disney tagline from Japanese characters to English.


In addition to the long-format videos, we produced :15 sec and :06 sec pieces that were used throughout social. For the :15 sec videos, custom haikus were created which communicated the past of Japan and the benefits of an Adventures by Disney trip in just 17 syllables. These worked as an extension of our long-form content and gave greater context to those who may not be familiar with the traditions and customs of Japan.


We extended the haiku approach to the static Pinterest content. We matched the common vertical typographic layout of authentic Japanese Haiku posters, but the poem itself was written in english.


The videos lead to an outstanding response from both past and future adventurers. The launch day was one of the best days Adventures by Disney ever had surpassing all projected booking estimates. In fact, there were twice as many calls as the 2016 itinerary launch and the company needed to add additional trip dates in order to keep up with the demand.
