What is fat babble?

Fat babble describes any statement that reinforces the thin-ideal standard of beauty and contributes to men and women's dissatisfaction of their bodies.

These are just a few of the examples of what fat babble looks like

Why it happens

Fat Talk has almost nothing to do with how you look, what you eat, or what you do in the gym. It has everything to do with how you feel about yourself, your life, and your circumstances.
These feelings come out as fat babble because it is a socially acceptable way of dealing with them, but it ends up hurting not only us, but the people around us as well.

Impact of fat babble

Fat babble reinforces the objectification of the self based on appearance.
