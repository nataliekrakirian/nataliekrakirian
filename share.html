Share your story

By sharing your story, you might be able to comfort someone who felt as if they were alone in how they felt, to inspire someone to stand up next time they hear fat babble, or even help yourself reflect on events that might have silently dug away at your self-esteem if they weren't confronted.

If you're not sure where to start, here are a few questions that will hopefully get the ball rolling.

  • What was said?
  • How did it affect you
  • Was this the first time that you have experienced fat babble in this specific manner?
  • Is there anything that you took away from the conversation?
  • Was there anything that you wished you had said?
So that we can inform you when your story has been posted.
