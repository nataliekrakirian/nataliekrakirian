3% Conference
Disney Print Ad

The background.

The 3% Conference is an event that brings people together to support the push for more female creative leadership in advertising agencies.

The challenge.

Create an awe-inspiring, impactful print ad that will showcase the diversity and creativity of The Walt Disney Company and speak to female creative talent.

The solution.

At The Walt Disney Company, we’re storytellers. Imagine if you were only given 3% of our beloved stories. The concept for this ad is exactly that. Through intense visuals showing only 3% of the story was told while the rest was torn away – we can display that at Disney, we need (and want) more than 3% to tell our stories. To pair with the 3% conference hashtag #ChangeTheRatio, we created #ChangeTheStory to tie into Disney's messaging for the event.
